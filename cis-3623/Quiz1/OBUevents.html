Events on Bison Hill

Oklahoma Baptist University values building community among students and the community at large. Listed below are some of the events that occur on Bison Hill during the fall semester.

The Weekend:

The Weekend is a long standing event established to welcome back alumni and families of current students. During the weekend students, their families and alumnus gather together on Bison Hill and share in making memories.

Stem Day:

Stem Day welcomes high school and middle school students to learn more about diverse STEM topics and gives them exposure to life on Bison Hill.
